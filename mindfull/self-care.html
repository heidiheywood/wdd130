Coping Strategies For You

Take Time to Care for You

These healthy habits are only beneficial to YOU if YOU use them. Be patient in your practice. New habits take time. Also Don't feel like you need to do all of these every day or week. These are best used in balance with our other responsibilities and roles that we fill.

Write Your Thoughts Down

Sometimes in order to process things better we need to get them out of our heads.

Pull Out Some Paint

It doesn't need to be a masterpiece just have fun.

Have a Dance Party With Yourself

I think everyone should have a mini dance party at least once a day.

Hop on a Bike

Exercise is a great way to recharge, reset and get some endorphins in your system.

Follow a Yoga Tutorial

Beginner or advanced, enjoy wherever you're at.

Spend a Few Minutes Cleaning

We often underestimate how our environment can impact how we feel.

Chat With a Friend

Do it. They'll appreciate hearing from you.

Watch Funny Animal Videos

It is incredible how much happiness watching cute animals can bring.

Be Still for 10 minutes

Practice bringing your attention back to the present moment and letting yourself fully experience it.

Listen to Music

Our moods can change with the music that we listen to so be intentional with the kind of music that you pick.

Meet Up With Friends

They might need it too :)

Go for a hike

Remember to take water
